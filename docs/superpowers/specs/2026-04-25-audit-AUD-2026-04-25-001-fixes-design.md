## Root Cause

The `!! wrap` / `!! ready` redesign migrated from a human-readable prose "summary" format to a terse machine-oriented `[SNAPSHOT]` block. The ops files (`session-memory.md`) were updated but `user-guide.md`, `setup-guide.md`, `README.md`, and `troubleshooting.md` were not fully updated, leaving stale terminology and behavioral descriptions throughout.
